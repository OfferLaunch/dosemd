Returns & Refund Policy

Last updated: February 12, 2026

✅ 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If a product doesn't meet stated purity specifications, we'll replace it or issue a full refund.

Our Guarantee

DoseMD stands behind the quality of every product we sell. All research peptides are accompanied by third-party certificates of analysis (COA). If any product does not meet its stated purity specifications, we will provide a full replacement or refund — no questions asked.

Eligible Returns

  • Unopened & sealed products may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund
  • Products that fail to meet stated purity specs are eligible for replacement or refund regardless of whether they've been opened
  • Products must be returned in their original packaging

Non-Eligible Returns

  • Opened products cannot be returned due to contamination risk and research integrity concerns
  • Products damaged due to improper storage by the buyer
  • Items beyond the 30-day return window

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return or report a quality issue:

  • Email support@dosemd.co with your order number and reason for return
  • Our team will respond within 24 hours with return instructions
  • Ship the product back using the provided return label (if applicable)

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect the returned product, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. You will receive an email confirmation when your refund has been issued.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or you receive the wrong product, contact us immediately at support@dosemd.co. We will expedite a replacement at no additional cost.
